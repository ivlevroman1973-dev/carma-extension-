CARMA Extension — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary: CARMA does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. All data stays on your device or is sent directly to Telegram via your own bot token.

1. What is CARMA?

CARMA is a Chrome extension that analyzes car listings on supported marketplaces (Encar, Mobile.de, Che168, and others), calculates import costs, and helps generate commercial proposals sent via Telegram.

2. Data We Collect

CARMA does not collect any personal data on our servers. The extension works locally in your browser.

5. Data Retention

All settings are stored in chrome.storage.local on your device only. No data is retained on any server operated by CARMA developers. You can clear all data by resetting settings or removing the extension.

6. Children's Privacy

CARMA is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
